Privacy Policy

Bible Notes & Study — Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. Local Data

Bible Notes & Study stores your reading position, notes, bookmarks, highlights, reading plans, search history, settings, and subscription access state on your device. We do not operate a developer-run account server, and the app does not require you to create an account.

2. iCloud Sync

iCloud sync is optional and only runs when you choose Sync to iCloud or Restore from iCloud. When enabled, the app writes or reads your study data in your private Apple iCloud container using Apple's iCloud services. The developer does not receive or operate a separate copy of this data.

3. Apple Services

Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. KJV Heritage Voice audio packs may be delivered as Apple-hosted On-Demand Resources. The app may contact Apple services for purchase validation, restore, iCloud sync, and audio resource delivery.

4. No Tracking or Ads

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social media SDKs, or cross-app tracking. We do not sell your data or use your reading activity for advertising.

5. Export

You can generate local exports from Settings. Markdown export is available for supported study data, and PDF export is a Plus feature. Exports are created on your device and shared only when you choose to share them.

6. Bible Content and Study Data

Bundled Bible text is included with the app. Study resources, notes, and reading data are stored locally unless you explicitly use iCloud sync. Optional study explanations currently use the selected verse text only and do not upload your notes or reading history.

7. Contact

If you have any privacy concerns, please email privacy@biblestudy.app.
