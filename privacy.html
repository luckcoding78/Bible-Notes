Privacy Policy

Bible Notes & Study — Last updated: May 31, 2025

1. Data Collection

Bible Notes & Study takes your privacy extremely seriously. We use a 100% local storage approach—your study notes, highlights, bookmarks, and reading plan progress are all saved within your device's local sandbox. We do not operate central servers, nor do we collect your personal habits or reading preferences in any form.

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is transmitted through Apple's encrypted channels to your private iCloud storage. The developers of Bible Notes & Study cannot access this data. The sync process is fully transparent and protected by Apple's highest-standard privacy agreements.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not contain any third-party analytics plugins, advertising SDKs, or social media integrations. We refuse to track users for commercial gain. The app only includes Bible text download services—these requests are sent directly to static resource servers and contain no personal sensitive information.

4. Data Export Rights

Your data always belongs to you. You can export your notes and records in Markdown or PDF format at any time through the "Export Data" feature in Settings. We promise not to place any barriers on data export.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

Bible Notes & Study Plus subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not directly collect, store, or process your credit card information. All payment data is securely managed by Apple and protected under Apple's Privacy Policy. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Bible Notes & Study is a pure reading tool suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of processing children's privacy data.

7. Contact

If you have any privacy concerns, please email privacy@biblestudy.app. We will respond within 48 hours.
